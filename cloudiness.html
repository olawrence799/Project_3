Latitude vs Cloudiness

The figure above plots the observed cloudiness (y-axis) against the city latitude (x-axis). While this plot does not suggest any strong correlation between latitude and cloudiness, it is worth noting that most data points lie below 40% or above 60% cloud cover. It's not so common to have cloudiness between 40% and 60%!
