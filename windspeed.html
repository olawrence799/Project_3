Latitude vs Wind Speed (mph)

The figure above plots the observed wind speed (y-axis) against the city latitude (x-axis). Like other plots contained in this site, there is not an obvious relationshop between this metric and the city's latitude. There isn't a city containted in this sample that both lies on the equator and registers much wind (high is about 12 mph). There are only two cities with wind measures exceeding 35 mph -- and those cities are at about -22 and 65 latitudes. Interestingly, cities with latitudes in the range 60-70 appear to have the widest range of sampled wind speeds.
